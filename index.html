So, what is DiSC?

DiSC is a model of human behaviour that helps people understand “why they do what they do”, how to recognise behaviour in others (what makes them tick), and how behaviour can be adapted to get better responses from others. Applying DiSC in the workplace helps teams understand one another and work better together.

Benefits of DiSC

DiSC is easy to understand, people get it quickly and can relate to it whatever their title, role or background.

DiSC provides a common language that we can use to discuss what behaviours we share with others and what we do differently.

DiSC is practical, after the Training workshop run by Kingsley you will be able to apply this knowledge immediately and see the difference.

The DiSC profile has been used over 50 million times, so you will be using a tool that is well researched and has worldwide credibility.
