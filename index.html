Reframe = positive change

Over the last few years many organisations have sought “silver bullets”, searching for the magic through amongst other things; CBT, NLP, Mindfulness, Emotional intelligence etc., but we believe to drive transformational, rather than transactional change then we need to address the individuals mind-set and work through the model below;

At Reframe we believe that everyone has the capacity to change their behaviour, and through that positive behaviour change, positive actions lead to strong performance.

Historically organisations have focused skills development around the actions that drive results, but we believe that by focusing on the individuals mind-set and their behaviour then positive change and reframing will happen.

We are passionate about people development, we are innovative in choosing tools and techniques and we look to ensure all our interventions are always memorable, meeting both the needs of the individual and the organisation.

We have a particular skill in training, coaching and mentoring experienced managers who have heard the theory but struggle to apply it in their work, and we have a deep empathy and understanding of the development needs of distance managers who work in geographic isolation.
